I'm an audio mixing engineer based out of Nashville, TN. Originally from Perth, Australia, I made the move to Music City in 2000 and haven't looked back. Over the past two decades I've had the privilege of mixing some of the most recognisable songs in Contemporary Christian Music — #1 radio records, platinum and gold certifications, Grammy-winning projects, and billions of combined streams.

My work spans CCM, country, and mainstream worlds. Whether it's a live worship record capturing a room full of thousands, or an intimate studio album, my philosophy is the same: the mix should serve the song, not the other way around.
